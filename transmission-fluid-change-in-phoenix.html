How long does a transmission fluid change take, and do I need to make an appointment?
Most transmission fluid changes at our Phoenix shop take about 1 to 2 hours. We recommend making an appointment to minimize your wait time, especially during our busy summer months. Walk-ins are welcome, but scheduling ahead ensures quicker service.
Is Pro-Tech Transmissions licensed and certified to perform transmission services in Arizona?
Yes, Pro-Tech Transmissions is fully licensed and insured in the state of Arizona. Our technicians are certified by the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) and have extensive experience specifically with transmission repairs and maintenance.
What is involved in a transmission fluid change at Pro-Tech Transmissions?
Our process includes draining the old transmission fluid, removing and cleaning or replacing the filter (if applicable), inspecting the pan for debris, and refilling with the manufacturer-recommended fluid. We also perform a complimentary multi-point transmission inspection to check for leaks or early signs of wear, which is especially important in Phoenix due to extreme heat.
